About AI

AI stands for
artificial intelligence
and refers to computer systems
that can perform tasks that typically require
human intelligence.
AI encompasses techniques such as machine
learning, deep learning, natural language processing, robotics, and computer vision.

Robotics

AI has made robots more

exploration, and manufacturing, by using

AI is used in education to personalize feedback for

student performance. It has potential to improve the learning experience and efficiency of educational institutions.

electronic health record mining, and medical advice through
chatbots.It can optimize hospital operations, but concerns around data privacy and bias must be addressed.
